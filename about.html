Our Story

Founded in 2009, Uber started as a simple idea: What if you could request a ride from your phone? Today, we've grown into a global platform connecting millions of riders and drivers.

We've expanded beyond rides to food delivery, freight transportation, and even urban air mobility, always with the same goal: to make transportation as reliable as running water, everywhere, for everyone.

Our Journey

2009

Uber is founded in San Francisco as "UberCab" by Garrett Camp and Travis Kalanick

2011

International expansion begins with launches in Paris, London, and Toronto

2014

UberEATS (now Uber Eats) launches as a food delivery service

2017

Uber Freight launches to connect shippers with truckers

2023

Operating in over 10,000 cities across 70 countries worldwide
